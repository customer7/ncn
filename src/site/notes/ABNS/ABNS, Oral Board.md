## Steps

- Pass ABNS written board during residency
- Complete ACGME-approved residency
- Register ABNS-POST^[[ABNS POST](https://abns.org/abnspost/)]
	- Logging cases could be tedious and time-consuming. 
		- Prepare to log cases ahead of time since cases can not be more than 24 months old at the time of submission.
		- Must submit before December 31st of the 3rd year from graduation of residency or fellowship (if has one)
- Must register oral board within 1 year of case log approval.
