**Thrombus**, AKA blood **clot**, can be classified into *red*, *white*, or *mixed*.^[[Differentiation of White, Mixed, and Red Thrombi: Value of CT in Estimation of the Prognosis of Thrombolysisâ€”Phantom Study | Radiology](https://pubs.rsna.org/doi/10.1148/radiol.2273020530?url_ver=Z39.88-2003&rfr_id=ori:rid:crossref.org&rfr_dat=cr_pub%20%200pubmed#REF4)]^[[Differentiation of white and red thrombus with magnetic reso... : Chinese Medical Journal](https://journals.lww.com/cmj/fulltext/2012/06010/differentiation_of_white_and_red_thrombus_with.10.aspx)]

- *Red* thrombus: erythrocytes and fibrin; responds well to thrombolytics.
- *White* thrombus: cell debris, fibrin, and platelets; few erythrocytes; resistant to thrombolytics.
